Ground Beef Tacos

Simple yet tasty ground beef tacos that are sure to make your mouth water

These simple tacos make for an easy dinner that does not take long, maybe even just 30 minutes before the food is only your table and ready to be assembled by your family or whoever you are going to be eating the tacos with

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 can (8 oz) tomato sauce
  • 12 hard or softshell 6" tortillas (corn or wheat)
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar, colby jack, or pepper jack cheese
  • 2 cups shredded lettuce
  • 2 medium tomatoes, chopped
  • 3/4 cup Old El Paso Thick n' Chunky Salsa
  • 3/4 cup Sour Cream(recommended but not required)

Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 250°F. In large skillet, brown ground beef and onion over medium heat for 8 to 10 minutes or until beef is thoroughly cooked, stirring frequently. Drain.
  2. Stir in chili powder, salt, garlic powder and tomato sauce. Reduce heat to low; cover and simmer 10 minutes.
  3. Meanwhile, place tortillas on ungreased cookie sheet. Heat at 250°F. for 5 minutes.
  4. To assemble tacos, layer beef mixture, cheese, lettuce and tomatoes in each tortilla. Serve with salsa; top with sour cream.
